Incline Dumbbell Curl

Target: Biceps Brachii (Long Head Focus)

Set a bench to a 45-degree incline. Let your arms hang straight down behind your torso. Keep your elbows locked in place as you curl the dumbbells upward, supinating your wrists at the top.

Biomechanics Note: Positioning the shoulder into hyperextension places the long head of the biceps into a passive stretch. Research shows training muscles at long muscle lengths significantly boosts hypertrophy.
